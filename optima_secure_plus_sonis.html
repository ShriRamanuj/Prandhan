Optima Secure+ · What You Must Know Before Buying
Waiting periods — complete guide
Every health policy has time periods during which certain conditions are not covered. Knowing these prevents claim surprises later.
Initial waiting
30 days
No illness claims in the first 30 days from policy start. Accidents are always exempt. Waived if you have 12+ months of prior continuous health cover.
Pre-existing diseases (PED)
36 months
Any condition diagnosed before policy start is excluded for 3 years. Portability credit reduces this — 2 years with previous insurer = only 1 year remaining here.
Specified diseases
24 months
40+ listed conditions — cataract, hernia, gallstones, kidney stones, joint replacement, varicose veins, hysterectomy and more — excluded for 2 years. Accidents exempt.
ABCD Chronic Care (add-on)
31st day
With this paid add-on, Asthma, BP, Cholesterol and Diabetes hospitalisations are covered from the 31st day — instead of 36 months. Most powerful waiting-period advantage in this product.
Maternity (Parenthood add-on)
24 months
Maternity, IVF and embryo storage covered 24 months after the Parenthood add-on is purchased. Must be opted at inception.
Moratorium period
60 months
After 5 years of continuous coverage, no claim can be challenged by the insurer on grounds of non-disclosure — except proven fraud. Your strongest legal protection.
Portability credit
Carry forward
Waiting periods served with your previous insurer are credited here under IRDAI portability norms. Apply at least 30 days before your renewal date.
Deductible waiver
After 5 years
If you opted for an aggregate deductible, you can waive it entirely (one-time) after 5 continuous years — provided the eldest insured is below 61 at that time. Premium adjusts accordingly.
Always disclose pre-existing conditions honestly
Non-disclosure can lead to claim rejection or policy cancellation. However, under IRDAI guidelines the insurer may instead offer to (a) permanently exclude the condition, (b) add up to 3 more years waiting, or (c) apply a loading — all with your acceptance. After 60 months continuous cover, even undisclosed conditions are protected except for fraud.
