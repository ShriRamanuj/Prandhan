Plan Comparison · Eligibility Guide
Who is each plan designed for?
Each plan has a very specific target profile. Choosing the wrong plan can mean claim rejections, excess co-payment or gaps in coverage. Read this section carefully.
Care Heart
🫀 Cardiac patients only
Ideal if you:
  • Have already undergone cardiac surgery — bypass (CABG), angioplasty, stent, valve replacement, pacemaker etc.
  • Were diagnosed with a cardiac ailment and had a surgical intervention for it
  • Want cardiac coverage to begin in just 2 years — not the standard 36 months
  • Want an annual cardiac health check-up cashless from Year 1
  • Can budget for 20% (age ≤70) or 30% (age >70) co-payment per claim
Do NOT buy if:
  • You have NOT had a cardiac procedure — you will not be eligible
  • You cannot manage mandatory co-payment from personal funds
  • You need higher SI (max is ₹10L) for very expensive treatments
Full Care Heart guide  ›
Senior Health Advantage
🧓 Seniors aged 60+ only
Ideal if you:
  • Are aged 60 or above — this is mandatory
  • Have multiple pre-existing conditions and most other insurers have refused coverage
  • Want no upper age limit and lifelong renewability
  • Need an annual health check-up (7 tests) from Day 1 — no waiting period
  • Have family support to fund the 50–60% co-payment when claims arise
Do NOT buy if:
  • You are under 60 — not eligible
  • The 50–60% co-payment is not financially manageable — that is a very large self-fund requirement
  • You want SI to grow — NCB is optional and Room is restricted
Full Senior guide  ›
Care Supreme Shine
✨ All ages — no restriction
Ideal if you:
  • Want genuine hospitalisation coverage at an affordable premium
  • Are comfortable with 20% co-payment — the lowest of the three
  • Are young and healthy and want a starting plan
  • Have corporate cover and want additional top-up protection
  • Walk regularly and want to earn up to 30% renewal discount
  • Plan to upgrade to full Care Supreme in 1–2 years
Do NOT buy if:
  • You want zero co-payment — upgrade to Care Supreme instead
  • You want coverage to grow each year — no CB in Shine
  • Multiple hospitalisations in one year are likely — no auto recharge
