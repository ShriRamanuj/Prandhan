Decision Tool
Help Me Decide
Answer these questions — and the right plan reveals itself
1. What is your primary goal in buying this policy?
🛡️ Replace my income / protect my family from loss of earning capacity → Koti Suraksha
👶 Ensure my child gets monthly income + education support if I die in an accident → Secure for Child
👨‍👩‍👧 Both — I want income protection AND child future protection → Buy Both
2. How much Accidental Death cover do you need?
₹30L–₹3 Crore → Secure for Child works (up to ₹300L)
₹2 Cr – ₹5 Crore → Koti Suraksha is the right choice (goes up to ₹5 Cr)
3. Do you want your Sum Insured to grow automatically each year without paying extra?
Yes, I want automatic SI growth → Secure for Child (Loyalty Benefit +5%/yr up to 50%)
No, I just want a fixed high cover → Koti Suraksha
4. Do you want Hospital Cash (daily allowance) during accident hospitalisation?
Yes, cash in hand every day I'm hospitalized → Koti Suraksha (₹3,000–₹5,000/day in-built)
Not a priority — I have health insurance for hospitalization → Secure for Child
5. Do you have elderly parents to look after?
Yes — and I want a benefit to take care of them if I'm disabled → Koti Suraksha (Parental Care Benefit ₹50K–₹1L)
No — my priority is my children's future → Secure for Child
