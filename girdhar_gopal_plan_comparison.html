🔑 Key Difference: Year 1 Cover
Optima Super Secure gives the highest first-year cover at ₹30L (2× Secure Benefit). Optima Secure+ gives ₹20L. Care Supreme base plan gives only ₹10L in Year 1 — significantly lower, though it catches up with Bonus Super add-on from Year 2 onwards.
📊 Long-Term Winner
Optima Secure+ is the only plan with truly unlimited growth (∞). Care Supreme's Bonus Super caps at 500% of SI (₹60L for ₹10L base). After Year 5, only Secure+ keeps growing. For a young family taking a 20-year view, Secure+ has no competition.
💡 Care Supreme's Unique Edge
Care Supreme offers "Unlimited Care" add-on — one lifetime claim with absolutely NO SI limit. This is a once-in-a-lifetime catastrophic cover feature not available in any HDFC plan. It also has the 30% wellness renewal discount, making it attractive for health-conscious families.
