⭐ Exclusive Specialisation

We Do One Thing — And We Do It With Mastery

In a world of generalist accountants, we have made a deliberate choice: to be the most skilled GST litigation firm in Kolhapur-Sangli district. From the first Show Cause Notice to the highest appellate tribunal, we stand with you at every stage — armed with deep case law knowledge, tribunal strategy, and a passion for justice.

📄

SCN Reply — Show Cause Notice

Precision drafting of replies to GST Show Cause Notices. Four-layer factual dissection, jurisdictional objections, without-prejudice pleadings, and blanket denial safeguards — all in one comprehensive reply.

🏛️

First Appeal — FAA (Appellate Authority)

Strategic appeal before the First Appellate Authority (Joint Commissioner Appeals). We draft multi-ground appeals covering natural justice, jurisdictional void, Section 75(7) penalty limits, and demand correctness.

⚖️

GSTAT — GST Appellate Tribunal

Full GSTAT appeal representation — SOF in 7-module format, grounds in 4-part anatomy, 10-clause prayer, and interlocutory stay applications. Expert oral hearing advocacy before the tribunal.

🔍

CESTAT Matters

Representation before the Customs, Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal for legacy service tax and customs matters that require experienced advocacy.

💡

GST Advisory & Classification

HSN classification disputes (Chapter 84, 55, etc.), refund strategy for inverted duty structure, ITC eligibility issues, and advance ruling support for complex transactions.

📊

Income Tax Litigation

SCN replies and appeals under the Income Tax Act 1961. MSME compliance advisory, Tax Audit representation, and Section 43B/MSME intersection issues.
